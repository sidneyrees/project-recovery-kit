## The 30-Minute Diagnostic

Ask these 4 questions:

1. Is scope an endless list? -> Scope creep. Freeze scope immediately.
2. Does the team not know what to do next? -> Missing PM. I become acting PM for 2 weeks.
3. Are deadlines constantly moving? -> Poor estimation. Switch to throughput-based planning.
4. Is business not aligned with tech? -> Translation gap. Create a shared glossary.

## My 5-Step Recovery Framework

1. Technical & Process Audit (48 hours)
2. Stakeholder Mapping & Expectation Reset
3. Stabilization Plan (ship one small thing in 2 weeks)
4. Controlled Execution & Daily Reporting
5. Roadmap to Scalability

## Real Recovery Case Study: Emergency Ecommerce Migration & Data Recovery

**Challenge**

A company contacted me during a critical business crisis. Their existing software provider had dramatically increased subscription fees, making the platform financially unsustainable. At the same time, the provider retained control of all business data, and no usable backup was available.

With more than **40,000 products**, **8,000 recurring customers**, and years of operational data locked inside the platform, the company faced the risk of losing access to essential business information and potentially being unable to continue operating.

**Solution**

I designed and executed an accelerated migration strategy focused on speed, data recovery, and business continuity.

* Developed custom Python bots to automatically scan, extract, and recover business data from the existing platform.
* Implemented a new WooCommerce infrastructure while the extraction process was still running.
* Optimized the platform to support a large catalog, customer base, and future growth.
* Performed data transformation, validation, and imports in parallel to minimize downtime.
* Coordinated the migration under extreme time constraints while ensuring data integrity.

**Results**

* Recovered and migrated **40,000+ products** and **8,000+ recurring customer records**.
* Completed the entire migration and platform launch in **7 days**.
* Restored full ownership and control of all business data.
* Prevented operational disruption during a highly time-sensitive transition.
* Reduced annual platform costs from an unsustainable level to a simple operating model consisting of standard hosting, domain registration, and approximately **10 hours of monthly support**.
* Delivered a scalable, open-source ecommerce platform that removed vendor lock-in and gave the company long-term independence.
